Overview

Purpose

To provide an easy to access space where temple patrons and visitors to the area can gain create bookings, information about the Temple Inn and Suites motel's ammenities and services. They will also be able to quickly access other information such as pricing, special events. and availability.

Audience

The audience for this site includes those planning trips to the area and specifically to the temples nearby. This person will be looking for a family friendly environment where they can easily have their needs met while visiting the temple, while simultaneously making lasting memories.
