Target Audience

Personas

Persona 1

  • Women aged 20-30
  • Received some college education, or finished bachelors degree
  • This person will be at the beginnings of her career
  • This person would access the site mostly for planning future activities for themselves and their families.
  • They would access the site on their mobile device most often

Persona 2

  • Women aged 15-20
  • In highschool
  • This person would be working part time or just starting to work full time
  • This person would access the site to plan activities for themselves, or to learn about weather in the surrounding areas
  • They would access the site only on their mobile device

Scenarios

Examples of why the audience may need the site

  • Is it going to rain later today when I plan to go to the park?
  • I saw a tik tok about a major storm happening on the East Coast, I wonder if it's near my sister's house in Pittsburgh
  • I want to schedule a play date for next week, what will be the best day for it?
  • The soonest I can get my air conditioner fixed is Thursday, is it going to be hot before then?
